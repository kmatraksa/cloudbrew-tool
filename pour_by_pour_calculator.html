Action Guide
CUT NOW — TDS_tail > 0.8%
Bitter/astringent compound concentration still high. Each additional gram drawn down adds disproportionately more astringency than sweetness. Lift dripper immediately.
MARGINAL — TDS_tail 0.4–0.6%
Bitterness declining, sweetness/floral increasing. Acceptable to include for some brews. For competition: cut at ~0.6% to eliminate astringency risk entirely.
SAFE — TDS_tail < 0.3%
Extract is highly dilute — neither bitter nor sweet compounds in significant quantity. Negligible impact on final cup. Drawdown may complete without quality risk.
Equivalency EY (EEY) Guide
EY_main < 19% — Tail cut is timely, below astringency zone
EY_main 19–21% — Equivalency threshold reached, cut earlier next brew
EY_main > 21% — Over-extracted, high astringency risk
